The Cutting Edge

Welcome to The Cutting Edge

We have our fingers (and shears) on the pulse of all the trendiest styles! We are a salon of professionally trained stylists in the Richmond area constantly honing our craft! We are well versed in a variety of hair types, from the fluffiest of kinks to the straightest tresses! From expert cuts, gorgeous color application, and the revitilization of damage hair; we do it all! Walk-ins welcome!
