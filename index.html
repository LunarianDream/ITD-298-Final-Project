Welcome to The Cutting Edge

We have our fingers (and shears) on the pulse of all the trendiest styles! We are a salon of professionally trained stylists in the Richmond area constantly honing our craft! We are well versed in a variety of hair types, from the fluffiest of kinks to the straightest tresses! From expert cuts, gorgeous color application, and the revitilization of damage hair; we do it all! Walk-ins welcome!

At The Cutting Edge we work with your hair, not against it. We prioritize the health and wellness of your scalp and hair, using negative ionic heat styling tools and wash and rinse with only filtered water to prevent any harsh mineral build up that may result from pure tap. This use of purified water leaves your hair bouncier and more easily accepting to moisture and product.

All of the products used within our salons have been formulated by the salon itself. At The Cutting Edge, it's imperative that our products are free of parabens and any harsh sulfates. Only the highest quality ingredients are sourced for our product production, so you can rest assured there's no harsh chemicals that only give the illusion of shiny healthy hair.

Trust us! We know that a visit to the salon can take up a large chunk of your day, and what better way to spend that time being pampered? At The Cutting Edge, we offer comfortable seating, snacks, and champagne (or sparkling water). We believe visiting a salon to be an act of self-care, and it is our goal to help you achieve that. These offerings are entirely complimentary, so need to feel as though you have to miss out experiencing luxury.
