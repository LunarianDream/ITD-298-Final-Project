My genuine honest opinions is that the course wasn't hard enough, and there should have been focused on something other than Dreamweaver as no one really uses it these days.

What I thought about the Web Design program in total and my time here at Reynolds

I enjoyed my time here at Reynolds. The professors were all easy to communicate with and very helpful when asked. The staff have also been really nice. I do wish the Web Design program gave more coding challenges, for example making us code design mockups.
